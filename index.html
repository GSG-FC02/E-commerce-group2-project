How we start

Athathy Company was luanched to supply elegant classic furniture for the local region. After two years, it has recently been grown to international company . Athathy is a lifestyle industrial design brand, which creates classically inspired leather, wood, and metal products. Another arm of the brand is Athathy Build, which focuses on architecture and design projects.
